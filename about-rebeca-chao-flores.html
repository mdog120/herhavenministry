A Little About Me

I was born in Dallas, Texas, and I go to the School for Science and Engineering at Townview Magnet Center. I’ve always loved music, movement, and serving others—whether that’s through dancing, reading with kids, or just being a calm, kind presence.

A big part of who I am is wanting people to feel supported and not alone. I like being behind the scenes, making sure details are taken care of so others can relax, breathe, and focus on what really matters.

Why I Serve in Her Haven

The heart behind saying “yes” to helping lead a spiritually-rooted club.

Faith • Care • Quiet Support

I wanted to be the Details & Resources Coordinator because I love helping others grow closer to God and serving my community in practical ways. The little things matter to me—like having what you need, feeling prepared, and knowing someone thought about you ahead of time.

Her Haven felt special because it wasn’t just another club; it was a space where girls could feel safe, encouraged, and spiritually filled. I knew I could support that by keeping information organized, helping with resources, and making sure our meetings and outreaches are clear and accessible.

My hope is that the work I do behind the scenes makes it easier for others to experience God’s love, find community, and feel welcomed the moment they walk in.

Life on Campus

On campus at Townview, I like staying involved in ways that let me be creative and caring at the same time. Music, movement, and serving others are all big parts of my everyday life.

  • • Choir: I love singing with others and using my voice—especially my high soprano notes—to bring joy and worship into spaces.
  • • Dance: Teaching dance at my church lets me connect with others, move freely, and express my love for God through creativity.
  • • Preschool Classroom Helper: Helping with little kids is one of my favorite things—they remind me to be gentle, patient, and joyful.
  • • Reading & Music: When I get a quiet moment, I love reading and listening to music, letting my mind and heart slow down and reset.
