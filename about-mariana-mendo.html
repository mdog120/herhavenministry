Born in Dallas, Texas, Mariana is a gentle leader with a heart that leans toward compassion, service, and community care. She attends the School of Science & Engineering with Morola and Isa, and brings a quiet but powerful presence to Her Haven’s mission.

Orchestra • Violinist Animal & Environmental Science Lover Faith + Service Driven

A Little About Me

I was born in Dallas, Texas, and I’ve always loved learning about the world around me—especially animals, ecosystems, and the little details that connect everything together.

I attend the School of Science & Engineering at Townview Magnet Center with Morola and Isa. Being part of a strong academic community has shaped my work ethic, my curiosity, and my desire to use what I’m learning to help others.

Why I Serve at Her Haven

The heart, intention, and calling behind her role.

I wanted to become an Outreach Coordinator because I love helping people and connecting with those who might feel unseen. Organizing projects, reaching out, and creating ways for us to support others makes me feel like I’m doing something that matters.

Being part of a ministry that not only talks about love but actually shows it through action and giving is meaningful to me. I’m grateful to help Her Haven reach more women, share hope, and build a community that cares.
