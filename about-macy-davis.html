A Little About Me

I was born in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, and now I go to the School of Science & Engineering at Townview Magnet Center. I’ve always loved reading and learning about what’s happening in the world—especially big issues that affect people’s everyday lives.

I’m part of Global Elementary Model United Nations (GEMUN), which lets me dive deeper into global topics and think about how we can respond. All of that connects back to my faith and my desire to use what I know to help others.

Why I Serve as Creative / Design Coordinator

The heart, the calling, and the reason she said “yes” to designing for the Kingdom.

Visual Storytelling • Faith • Advocacy

I wanted to be the Creative / Social Media Coordinator because I want to spread God’s word in any way I can—especially through visuals, words, and designs that catch people’s attention and point them back to Him.

Learning about world issues and being involved in GEMUN has shown me how broken the world can be, but also how important it is to speak up, raise awareness, and care. Her Haven gives me a place to combine that with my faith.

I also love working with kids at my church and helping them grow closer to God. Designing for Her Haven feels like another way to do that—creating things that help people see who He is, how He loves, and why women matter so much to His heart.
