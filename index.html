The Vision: Our Goal for Your Growth

Her Haven is a structured, Christ-centered space designed to generate enough funds for women in abusive relations. 1 in 3 women in Texas are currently in abusive relationships, and 1 in 5 in the United States.

Our mission is simple: to spread the love of Jesus in our community through gathering donations for women's shelters!

Our Core Focus: The 4 Pillars of Growth

  • I.
    Service That Actually Matters: Semester-long, deeply involved service projects. Forget one-off hours; we're talking tangible, long-term community transformation.
  • II.
    Deepening Your Faith: Bi-weekly, faculty-led Bible studies that move beyond surface level—focused on applying Biblical truth to leadership and daily life.
  • III.
    Real-World Leadership Skills: We put you in charge! Get hands-on experience in budgeting, event planning, and communication—the skills employers look for.
  • IV.
    Authentic Sisterhood & Support: Confidential, zero-judgment wellness sessions and formal mentorships to build a supportive network that lasts beyond graduation.
