A Little About Me

I was born in Dallas, Texas, and I go to the School of Science and Engineering at Townview Magnet Center. I’m definitely a “music in my headphones, weights in my hands” kind of girl—anything that has to do with music or working out makes me happy.

I care a lot about growth—physically, mentally, and spiritually. I’m kind of obsessed with my body goals, but not just for looks. I love feeling strong, disciplined, and confident, and I want other girls to feel that too.

Why I Serve as Service Coordinator

The story behind saying “yes” to helping lead Her Haven from the inside.

Faith • Community • Calling

I wanted to be a Service Coordinator because I thought it would be such a cool opportunity to spread our faith and actually recruit people into a real community—not just a club you show up to, but a space that feels like a sisterhood.

I love the idea that we can invite girls in, serve together, and show them what it looks like to live out what we believe. Planning service projects, organizing who does what, and helping things run smoothly feels like the perfect mix of leadership and heart.

My goal is to help build a community where girls can grow in their faith, in their confidence, and yes, in their goals—inside and out.

Life on Campus

On campus, my life is pretty much a mix of classes, playlists, and workouts. I like staying busy and working toward goals, whether that’s in school, at the gym, or in my relationship with God.

  • • Music Everything: I love anything that has to do with music—listening, singing along, finding songs that fit every mood.
  • • Working Out: Lifting, moving, training—if it’s active, I’m in. I’m serious about my body goals and I love the process of getting stronger.
  • • Faith & Discipline: For me, discipline in the gym and discipline in my faith go together. Both take consistency, sacrifice, and heart.
